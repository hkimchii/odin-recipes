Bibimbap

Description

Bibimbap is one of the most well known Korean dishes. A rice bowl topped with all sorts of seasoned saut√©ed vegetables, marinated meat (usually beef), a fried egg sunny side up, finished with a sprinkly of sesame and generous dollop of a sweet-spicy-savoury Bibimbap sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of cooked white rice (preferably short grain)
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons sesame seeds
Korean beef & marinade:
  • 250g beef tenderloin or thick steak (very finely sliced)
  • 1/4 green apple (grated)
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey (or brown sugar)
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
Vegetables:
  • 2 carrots (cut into slim batons)
  • 2 zucchini (cut into slim batons)
  • 1 bunch of spinach (cut into 5cm lengths)
  • 8 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 4 cups bean sprout
  • 2 teaspoons garlic (minced)
  • 8 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon fish sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon white sugar
  • Sesame oil
Bibimbap sauce:
  • 4 tablespoons gochujang paste
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 3 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 garlic clove (finely grated)
  • 2 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. Bibimbap sauce. Mix ingredients until sugar is dissolved.
  2. Marinate beef. Mix the marinade in a bowl, then add beef. Marinate for 30 minutes to overnight.
  3. Cook marinated beef. Heat 2 tsp oil in a large skillet over high-heat. Let excess marinade drip off then add beef. Cook for 3 - 4 minutes until cooked and there's some caramelised bits, then remove from skillet. Keep warm until required or reheat to warm.
  4. Prepare shiitake mushrooms.Soak mushrooms in a large bowl of boiling water for 30 minutes, or until rehydrated. Drain, squeeze out excess water, then slice.
  5. Carrot and Zucchini salting. Place carrot and zucchini in separate bowls, sprinkle each with 1/4 tsp salt, toss, leave for 20 minutes then drain excess liquid.
  6. Cook shittake mushrooms. Heat 2 tsp oil in a skillet over meidum-high heat. Cook mushrooms for 2 minutes. Add 1.5 tsp soy, 1/4 tsp sugar of garlic. Stir for 1 minute, then remove.
  7. Cook carrot. Add 2 tsp oil into the skillet, cook carrot until just tender (5 to 8 minutes), then remove.
  8. Cook zucchini. Cook as with carrot for 4 minutes.
  9. Cook spinach. Heat 2 tsp veg with a splash of sesame oil. Saute until starting to wilt. Add 1/2 tsp garlic, and salt to taste, stir, then remove. When cool, squeeze to drain out excess liquid.
  10. Cook beansprouts.Simmer in water for 5 min or steam in microwave for 3 min until floppy. Drain under cold water, then cool. Squeeze out excess liquid with hands, place in bowl. Mix with 2 tsp sesame oil, 1 tsp garlic, 1/4 tsp fish sauce.
  11. Assemble. Fry eggs in a skillet to your taste. Place warm rice in bowls. Top with vegetables and beef, as pictured in post, then lastly, the egg. Sprinkle with sesame seeds, drizzled with sesame oil. Serve with Bibimbap sauce!
